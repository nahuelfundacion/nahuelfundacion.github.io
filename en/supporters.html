How it works

Simple registration and bank transfer.

At this first stage, supporter registration is handled through the institutional email. Each person indicates basic information, the monthly amount they wish to contribute and a tentative payment date.

The foundation then confirms registration and provides or validates the institutional bank details. Monthly contributions can be programmed as recurring transfers from each supporter’s bank.

Contribution details

Institutional bank transfer.

For operational security, full bank details are confirmed by institutional email during registration. Contributions should not be sent to personal accounts.
